Proposal day

29 Dec 2020

It was a cold winter evening when Randy suggested to Amanda that they should take a drive downtown to look at all the Christmas lights and Christmas trees before they get taken down. Amanda’s brother and mom decided to come for the ride too. After a quick walk around Nathan Philips Square. They started to make there way back home. Amanda saw a beautiful Christmas tree as they passed the Bay-Adelaide Center. She just had to stop to take a picture, so Randy pulled over. As they walked through the many lit up trees and sparkling lights, Amanda turned back looking for Randy to only find him in front of the beautiful Christmas tree down on one knee with a ring in his hand. “We have been together for 7 years now and I want to make it forever. Will you marry me?” Of course Amanda said YES! Our favourite holiday is Christmas so this made it extra special.
